Research Vision & Trajectory

Trustworthy Intelligent Systems for
Complex, Data-Rich Environments

My research programme is centred on the design of trustworthy intelligent systems for complex, data-rich, and uncertainty-prone environments. It builds on foundational work in uncertainty modelling, information fusion, and knowledge discovery from geospatial data (Ph.D., Rennes 1; HDR, Manouba), and has evolved toward scalable deep learning, computer vision, privacy-preserving AI, federated learning, and IoT security.


More recently, my work has moved toward the convergence of edge intelligence, multimodal AI, and trustworthy autonomous systems, with growing interest in agentic AI for distributed decision-making. As Director of the RIOTU Laboratory, I am advancing research integrating remote sensing, IoT, machine learning, computer vision, and language-enabled AI to develop robust solutions for precision agriculture, environmental monitoring, intelligent healthcare, and autonomous unmanned systems.

Signature Research Contributions

Five Research Lines Defining This Programme

1

Uncertainty Modelling & Geospatial Intelligence

Established uncertainty-aware modelling, information fusion, and knowledge discovery from satellite and geospatial data — the methodological foundation for trustworthy AI.

Foundation for 3 doctoral theses and the HDR qualification
2

Scalable Deep Learning & Computer Vision

Advanced distributed deep learning architectures for large-scale visual analytics in remote sensing, addressing bottlenecks in memory, scale, and modelling complexity.

Top-cited papers in CEA & Ecological Informatics
3

Privacy-Preserving & Trustworthy AI

Contributed to privacy-aware, federated, and secure learning pipelines for sensitive datasets in healthcare and remote sensing — showing decentralization need not compromise utility.

Directly applicable to defense and health sectors
4

Translational AI for Precision Agriculture

Developed AI-driven Red Palm Weevil detection from acoustic signals and UAV monitoring. Saudi patent (SAIP SA 18938 B1, 2024).

Under commercial evaluation — hundreds of millions in economic impact
5

Emerging: LLMs, Generative AI & Autonomous Systems

Research at the convergence of large language models, generative AI, edge intelligence, and autonomous unmanned systems. Complemented by widely cited survey papers consolidating emerging areas.

Book: "The Rise of Agentic Intelligence" (Springer, 2026)
